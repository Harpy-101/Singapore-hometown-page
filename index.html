Singapore

Top three activities to do in Singapore

A serene escape from the bustling city, the Singapore Botanic Gardens offers lush greenery, tranquil lakes, and the world-renowned National Orchid Garden.

Experience luxury and innovation at Marina Bay Sands, with its iconic infinity pool offering breathtaking views of Singapore’s skyline.

Explore a futuristic forest at the Supertree Grove, where towering, tree-like structures come alive with a dazzling light and sound show each evening.

Feel the adrenaline of the Singapore F1 GP, the world’s only night race set against the stunning backdrop of Marina Bay, blending high-octane action with world-class entertainment.
